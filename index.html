Marzieh Fadaee

PhD candidate, University of Amsterdam

I am a PhD candidate in Information and Language Processing Systems (ILPS) group working on developing models to understand interesting phenomena of the natural language.

My research includes exploiting paraphrase relations for improved translation modeling, adapting the embedding space to capture the ambiguity nature of the language using topics, translation of infrequent words with neural systems, and idiom translation.

I work under the supervision of Christof Monz and Arianna Bisazza. I received my B.Sc. from Sharif University of Technology majoring in Computer Engineering and M.Sc. from University of Tehran.
